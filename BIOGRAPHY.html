I was born and raised in San Miguel, Bulacan, a peaceful town where everyday life felt simple and comforting. My childhood was filled with small adventures that shaped how I look at the world today. I grew up in a family that valued respect, faith, and hard work. Every morning, nagigising ako sa tilaok ng mga manok at tunog ng sasakyan that are passing to our house. I enjoyed spending time outside, sa paglalaro ng taguan, tumbang preso and many more with friends in our barangay. We often used old slippers and tin cans for our games, making the fun feel more authentic. Umaakyat pa kami sa matataas na puno noon, namimitas ng prutas, and raced each other in wide empty fields. I loved going to the palengke with my parents dahil palagi akong nagpapabili ng laruan doon. Our community was close-knit, and neighbors treated each other like family. At home, I learned responsibility through simple chores. Even at a young age, I grew fond of computers and gadgets, which sparked my curiosity. Looking back, ang mga karanasan ko sa Bulacan ang siyang tumulong sakin to build the discipline and curiosity I still carry today.
